color
coral · #f85a3c
ink · #252220
paper · #fdfcfb
  • Coral marks the tabletop and small accents. It is never a surface — the logo does not appear on, or as, a full-coral field, so overlap with other orange brands is not a concern.
  • Seats and wordmark inherit the text color; use the mono variant on coral-adjacent or busy grounds.
  • One ink plus coral, at most. No gradients, no outlines, no shadows.

  • tuck-in — seats tuck flush into the tabletop — is the baseline hover, used on the navbar logo.

  • These two are the entire motion vocabulary; do not invent new logo animations.
